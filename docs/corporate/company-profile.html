Our Services

Market Sphere Group delivers structured, client-focused services designed to create measurable impact for individuals, businesses, and communities.

  • Entrepreneurship Development Training and enterprise support that fosters the spirit of entrepreneurship and reduces unemployment and Government dependency.
  • Theory of Music Basic music education and theory instruction for learners building foundational musical knowledge and creative skills.
  • Career Development Career guidance delivered through structured projects and mentorship for professionals and young people entering the workforce.
  • Academic Tutoring Flexible academic tutoring for school-going and out-of-school learners seeking to upgrade grades and build confidence.
  • Youth Empowerment Projects & Mentorship Youth-centred projects and mentorship programmes that harness the potential of young people across Botswana.
  • Platform Mass Marketing Digital platforms and tools that give businesses and service providers broader reach, faster visibility, and meaningful customer engagement.
  • Basic Farming Practice Practical farming awareness and basic agricultural practice support for communities and emerging agri-entrepreneurs.
  • Real Estate Services We, together with our partner company, help with valuation reports, sales, rentals, and property management countrywide.

Digital Marketplace Platform

Our online platform connects customers with verified service providers across Botswana. Users can browse by category and location, view detailed provider profiles, and submit enquiries directly through a secure, professionally managed environment.

Every provider application is reviewed by our team before going live, ensuring quality, trust, and accountability across the network. The platform reflects our commitment to innovation, professionalism, and customer satisfaction.

Market Sphere Group (Pty) Ltd stands as a registered Botswana enterprise committed to delivering practical, people-centred solutions that strengthen individuals, businesses, and communities. Through our integrated portfolio — spanning entrepreneurship development, education, career guidance, youth empowerment, digital marketing, basic farming practice, and real estate services — we work to reduce unemployment, build skills, and create lasting economic opportunity across Botswana and the wider SADC region.
